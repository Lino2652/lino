About This Event

 

The world's food system faces unprecedented challenges. A growing population, a changing climate, and resource limitations demand innovative solutions. Here at Federal University of Technology, Owerri, we believe that the future of food lies in embracing technology and fostering collaboration across disciplines. That's why the School of Information Technology (SICT) is proud to present Future of Food, a one-day event that dives deep into the intersection of Food, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Vertical Farming. Get ready to explore the cutting-edge advancements that are transforming how we produce, distribute, and consume food in a more sustainable and efficient way.
